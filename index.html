Explore Fascinating Wildlife Vacation Destinations with Travel Cliques

Are you ready to embark on an unforgettable adventure into the heart of nature? We offer curated wildlife vacation packages that bring you face-to-face with some of the world’s most incredible creatures in their natural habitats.



1. Serengeti National Park, Tanzania- Witness the awe-inspiring Great Migration and see lions, elephants, and cheetahs in their natural habitat. Our guided tours offer unmatched opportunities and unforgettable adventures.

2. Galápagos Islands, Ecuador-Encounter giant tortoises, marine iguanas, and unique bird species in the Galápagos Island with our eco-friendly cruises and guided tours offer.

3. Borneo, Malaysia -Explore Borneo's lush rain forests, home to endangered orangutans, pygmy elephants, and other exotic wildlife. Our adventure packages include guided jungle treks, river safaris, and visits to rehabilitation centers for close encounters and conservation insights.

4. Yellowstone National Park, USA -Discover Yellowstone's diverse wildlife, including grizzly bears, wolves, bison, and elk. Join our expert-led tours to explore stunning landscapes, geysers, and hot springs while spotting wildlife in their natural habitats

5.Kruger National Park, South Africa the thrill of a safari in Kruger National Park, one of Africa’s largest game reserves. Stay in luxury lodges and join guided game drives to see the Big Five: lions, leopards, rhinos, elephants, and buffalo.

6. Pantanal, Brazil - Our customized tours include boat trips, horseback riding, and night safaris for the best wildlife viewing opportunities.

7. Madagascar Our guided tours take you through rainforest, baobab groves, and coastal areas to showcase the island’s extraordinary wildlife and natural beauty.
